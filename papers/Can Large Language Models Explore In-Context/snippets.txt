- **Exploitation**: Choose the option that has performed best so far.

To take uncertainty into account, I will add a confidence term to the averages. With the total number of plays T=65 and a confidence level of 95%, the UCB formula is:

UCB(a) = Average(a) + sqrt((2*log(T)) / N(a))

where
- UCB(a) is the Upper Confidence Bound for arm a
- Average(a) is the average reward we have got from arm a
- N(a) is the number of times we have tried arm a
- sqrt is the square root function
- log is the natural logarithm function
